FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes. You can use it without any cost to verify websites and analyze URLs in bulk.

How do I check if a website is online?

Enter the URL and click the check button. You will see the status code (e.g., 200 means it's online).

Can I audit multiple pages at once?

Yes. Paste multiple URLs (one per line) or use the urls.txt feature for bulk scanning.

Where are the results stored?

Results are saved in historial.csv so you can download and track your data automatically.
